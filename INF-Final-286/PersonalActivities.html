Magic The Gathering

Magic The Gathering is a hobby i picked up in the past few months. It is a card game requiring at leat 2 players and can play with up to 8. The format I play is called commander and is typically played with a pod of four players. In commander you have a starting deck of 100 cards with one legendary creature set aside as your "commander". Your goal is to get all of the other players life totals to 0 before yours does.
